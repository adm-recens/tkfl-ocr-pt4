ML Model Training Hub

This page serves as the control center to utilize the human-validated data (from the **View Receipts** page) to retrain and fine-tune the core OCR and data extraction ML models (PyTorch/FastAPI).

Training Roadmap (Future Feature)

The validated data is ready for model training, but the execution interface is under development. Here's what's coming:

  • **Data Preparation:** Automatic generation of annotated training data from validated receipts.
  • **Configuration:** Define training parameters (epochs, learning rate) and target models.
  • **API Execution:** Call to a dedicated FastAPI service for **asynchronous** model training.
  • **Monitoring:** Dashboard for tracking training progress (loss, accuracy, time remaining).
